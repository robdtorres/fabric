Fabric is our design system for all ComplianceMetrix products going forward. Adopted from best practice and designed for our specific needs, Fabric is an homage to Google's Material Design System. The design system is based on Material Design for Bootstrap (which is built over a full Bootstap 4 foundation).

Fabric, though a synonym to Material, has a much bigger meaning for ComplianceMetrix. Fabric is quite literally meant to be our design system - woven into the core platform experience - that our squads can build upon.

The primary component of Fabric will be the Fabric Pattern Library (based on the Pattern Lab Atomic Design System). It will house all of ComplianceMetrix's core product patterns, templates and more. The goal is to enable our squads to access the same core library of UI to build upon consistently.
